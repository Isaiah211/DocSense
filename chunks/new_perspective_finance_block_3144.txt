Financial markets may also design such mechanisms to deter illegal buying and selling of

Weinberg and Kruger (2000) have pointed out that exchanges now pay brokerage firms for funnelling
orders to the exchanges.5
 This illustrates that financial markets, now fiercely competing with each
other, are using any business strategy available. With the rapid growth in online brokerage firms and
trading platforms, financial markets, in trying to make profits, are paying for the order flow.
