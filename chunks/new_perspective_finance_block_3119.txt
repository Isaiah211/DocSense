We think that the proliferation or failure of e-money depends not only on the
customer acceptance (though crucial), but also on the firms’ incentives to develop and promote this
new scheme. Firstly, the issuer of e-money commits to provide specified quantities of goods at any future time,
which can be either instantaneous or far into the future. As a special type of debt, e-money can be
used as a source of financing, especially for operation.
