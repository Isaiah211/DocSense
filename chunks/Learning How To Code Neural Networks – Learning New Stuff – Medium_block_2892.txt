This process happens backwards, because you start at the end of the network (observe how wrong the networks ‘guess’ is), and then move backwards through the network, while adjusting the weights on the way, until you finally reach the inputs. To calculate this by hand requires some calculus, as it involves getting some derivatives of the networks’ weights.
