After this training, the model was able to drive the car by itself on the first track for hours and generalized to the second track. All the training was based on driving data of about 4 laps using ps4 controller on track 1 in one direction alone. The model never saw track 2 in training, but with image augmentation (flipping, darkening, shifting, etc) and using data from all the cameras (left, right and center) the model was able to learn general rules of driving that helped translate this learning to a different track.
