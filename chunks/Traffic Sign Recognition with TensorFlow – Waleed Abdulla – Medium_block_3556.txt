The output of the fully connected layer is a logits vector of length 62 (technically, it’s [None, 62] because we’re dealing with a batch of logits vectors). A row in the logits tensor might look like this: [0.3, 0, 0, 1.2, 2.1, .01, 0.4, ....., 0, 0]. The higher the value, the more likely that the image represents that label.
