Dewan and
Mendelson (1998) study time-based competition in imperfect securities markets, where traders make
IT investments to gain faster access to information and thus earn higher trading profits. As well as the market microstructure literature, there are other studies that have shed considerable
light on the market mechanism. Shiller (1993) pointed out that the market system is an invention, a
system created by social thinkers who designed it with a purpose.
