You’ve probably heard about Google’s self driving cars, but Tesla, Mercedes, BMW and other car manufacturers are also making big bets on self driving technology. Regulations, not technology, are the primary obstacles for drone-based commercial services such as Amazon air, and just a few days ago, the FAA relaxed restrictions on commercial drone flights. It’s still not legal for Amazon to deliver packages to your door with drones, but that will soon change, and when that happens, commerce will never be the same.
