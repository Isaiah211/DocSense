During this process, each Pin accumulates numerous unique textual descriptions which allows us to connect Pins with interests terms with high precision. However, this conceptually simple process requires non-trivial engineering effort to scale to the amount of Pins and Pinners that the service has today. The data process pipeline (managed by Pinball) composes over 35 Hadoop jobs, and runs periodically to update the user-interest mapping to capture users’ latest interest information.
