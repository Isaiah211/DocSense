Structured data enables the reader to follow certain topics or stories, improves search and enables timeline navigation of a news story to help the reader better understand the context of the story and how it has developed. At the same time, a journalist writing a story on the uproar in Ukraine has no possible way of knowing how the story will unfold in the weeks to come. Manual tagging of news stories leads to inconsistent and incomplete structures due to a subjective understanding of which topics are important and related.
