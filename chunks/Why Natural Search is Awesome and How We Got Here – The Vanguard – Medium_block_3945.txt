In 2012–2013 we were building Desti — a holistic travel search app (i.e. it would search for everything — from hotels through attractions to restaurants), using post-Siri natural-language-understanding tech, and with powerful semantic search capabilities on the back end that allowed Desti to reason meaningfully about search results and make highly informed suggestions. Desti’s search was built on a premise that sounds very simple, but it’s actually very hard to pull off.
