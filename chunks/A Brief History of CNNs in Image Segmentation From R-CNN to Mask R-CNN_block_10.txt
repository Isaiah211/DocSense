R-CNN does what we might intuitively do as well - propose a bunch of boxes in the image and see if any of them actually correspond to an object. R-CNN creates these bounding boxes, or region proposals, using a process called Selective Search which you can read about here. At a high level, Selective Search (shown in the image above) looks at the image through windows of different sizes, and for each size tries to group together adjacent pixels by texture, color, or intensity to identify objects.
