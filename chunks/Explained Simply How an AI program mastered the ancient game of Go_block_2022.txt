For this paper, as I lightly mentioned in the Abstract and also underlined in the screenshot above, the biggest innovation was the fact that they used RL with neural networks for improving an already popular game-playing algorithm, MCTS. RL is a cool tool in a toolbox that they used to fine-tune the policy and value function neural networks after the regular supervised training. This research paper is about proving how versatile and excellent this tool it is, not about teaching you how to use it.
