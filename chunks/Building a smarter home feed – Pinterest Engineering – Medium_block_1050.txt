Smart feed is a composition of three independent services, each of which has a specific role in the construction of a home feed. The smart feed worker is the first to process Pins and has two primary responsibilities — to accept incoming Pins and assign some score proportional to their quality or value to the receiving user, and to remember these scored Pins in some storage for later consumption. Essentially, the worker manages Pins as they become newly available, such as those from the repins of the people the user follows.
